Residual Calculation Approach

Residuals were calculated using:

Residual = Actual Sales − Predicted Sales

Predicted Sales were generated using the final multiple regression model.

Positive residuals indicate the model under-predicted sales.

Negative residuals indicate the model over-predicted sales

Business Interpretation

The residual analysis shows that while the regression model explains a large portion of sales variation, it does not capture every factor affecting store performance.

Some stores consistently outperform expectations while others underperform expectations. This indicates that leadership should use the regression model as a decision-support tool rather than a perfect forecasting tool.

The model appears generally balanced and does not show evidence of systematic over-prediction or under-prediction across the full dataset.
